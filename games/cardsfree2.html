New Solitaire

New Solitaire is a fun card game in the tradition of the good old Solitaire card games series. The aim of the game is to use all the cards from the stacks in order to build up four suit stacks in ascending order, beginning with the aces. Double-click on any aces on the eight stacks to move them to the spaces above at the suit stack. Make the plays available to you on the board. Build row stacks and suit stacks to free up cards that you need to build the suit stacks.

To move a card or a stack of cards, from one row to another, click and drag the card or stack. To move a card from a row stack to a suit stack, double-click on it. Click the next freed underlying card to turn it over. When a row stack is open (no cards in the row), you can move a king (along with any cards that might be in its stack) to the open row stack. When you have no moves left, click reset to start again. Click 'Finish' when you finish building the suit stacks. Good luck!
