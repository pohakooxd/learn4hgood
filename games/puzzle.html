10 Blocks is deceptively simple! Although it takes its inspiration from Tetris, it is quite the brain teaser - and at times even more difficult.

This game breaks the rule of Tetris and essentially gives you all the control. No more blocks careening down at you, now you can choose where to put them. Simple! You start with 3 pieces you need to put in an open square space. After you place them, you immediately get 3 more pieces you also have to fit into that square space. Soon you're running out of space and the grey screen hits you with a terrible âno more moves' sign. Despite having more control, it seems to be even harder than Tetris!

It does, thankfully, lend some other rules from Tetris. If you close a line with blocks that line will disappear, giving you some space to fill up again. But you'll notice it takes a little longer to do than in Tetris, and you'll struggle to fit them in to get that cathartic disappearing line.
