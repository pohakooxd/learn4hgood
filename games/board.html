Casual Checkers

In Casual Checkers, you can play the classic age old board game online! Move your checkers diagonally forwards, aiming to get to the last line of the board to turn your pieces into queens (which can move backwards as well as forwards). You can only move one square at once, but if you have the chance to leap over an enemy piece to a blank square in a straight line, you will be able to capture that piece! Casual Checkers has several customizable features — choose from three different coloured boards, as well as turning on ‘forced captures (where you have to capture a piece if possible), and changing the difficulty (easy-medium-hard).

Domino Legend

Domino Legend is a super cool casino-like domino game. The game is played by the opposing player (the computer) placing a domino into the middle of the table,you must match the number on their domino with one of your own. If you can't match, you have to pick dominoes up until you can match. The round is over when one player places all of their tiles down, or the game is blocked and no one else can move.
