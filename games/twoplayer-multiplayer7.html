Carrom

Carrom: 2 Player is an online digital version of the classic Indian board game, Carrom. In this game, you play against a friend in your duel, both aiming to pocket your pieces before your opponent.
Carrom is a similar game to billiards. It has many rules, but your main aim is to remove pieces by hitting them into pockets with your hit piece. You should hit the pieces of your color, which is assigned as the first color you manage to get into the pocket. Each piece is worth five points. Beware of hitting your hit piece into the pocket, as this is a foul and will deduct 10 points from your tally!
