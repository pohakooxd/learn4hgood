Scaled

Scaled is a new look at a puzzle game, where you're trying to cut off the bouncing ball into a small square area. Sounds simple? Think again.

In Scaled you have an area where a ball is bouncing, you systematically cut down the size of the block until you've reached the sweet spot and complete the level. Although it sounds simple, it is far more complex. Each level you start with a token with a cut-type on it, like a line or a corner. When you put it down, that token will then slowly begin to cut straight out until it reaches the walls of the main block and then cut that part off. However, should the ball touch the cutting-line at any point, it stops, and you lose that token (and a life) and get a new one. So timing is extremely important in this game. The levels get more and difficult until you have two balls you have to cut off, and that will drive you bouncing mad.
