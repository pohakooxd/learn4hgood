Divide

Divide is an entertaining game where you have to use the separators you are given to clear the space where the ball is in. Once you clear enough, it will reset to full size and you can continue, you get a point for every “reset” you have. Make sure the ball doesn’t touch the separators.

How to Play: Drag and place the separators where you want them to be. Once they are in place, the ball can’t touch the lines they create, so make sure you have enough space. It’s easier to clear big chunks of the area to clear it fast. If you create too many corners and such, the ball will bounce in all directions and make it harder to divide the level. In order to score a point, you need to enclose the ball in a rectangle.

The game can be played on PC and mobile. Use your mouse or touch your screen to place the separator and see how many areas you can close.
